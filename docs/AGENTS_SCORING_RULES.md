# Scoring Rules

Target score sources:

- color lines
- type lines
- Royal Lines
- combo chains
- survival duration
- tactical efficiency

Small negative adjustments may come from sacrificing or capturing valuable pieces.

Piece value bands:

- common: 2
- medium: 4-6
- rare: 8

Best scores should come from:

- low sacrifice play
- efficient positioning
- controlled survival
- elegant tactical decisions

Keep scoring readable and player-legible.

Avoid hidden bonuses that the player cannot understand.
